# Thương lượng — Từ vựng & Hội thoại mẫu

## Đề xuất ý tưởng với sếp

| Câu | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| I'd like to propose... | Tôi muốn đề xuất... |
| What if we tried...? | Nếu chúng ta thử... thì sao? |
| I think this could help us... | Tôi nghĩ điều này có thể giúp... |
| The benefit of this approach is... | Lợi ích của cách này là... |
| I've seen this work well in... | Tôi đã thấy điều này hiệu quả ở... |
| Would you be open to...? | Bạn có sẵn lòng xem xét... không? |

## Thương lượng scope / deadline

| Câu | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| Can we prioritize...? | Chúng ta có thể ưu tiên... không? |
| If we cut X, we can deliver Y on time | Nếu bỏ X, chúng ta có thể giao Y đúng hạn |
| What's the minimum viable version? | Phiên bản tối thiểu khả thi là gì? |
| Can we phase this out? | Mình có thể chia giai đoạn không? |
| I want to be transparent about the risk | Tôi muốn minh bạch về rủi ro |
| We can do X, but we'll need to push Y | Chúng ta có thể làm X, nhưng Y sẽ bị trễ |

## Xin tăng lương / thăng chức

| Câu | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| I'd like to discuss my compensation | Tôi muốn thảo luận về lương |
| Based on my contributions, I believe... | Dựa trên đóng góp của tôi, tôi tin... |
| I've taken on additional responsibilities | Tôi đã đảm nhận thêm trách nhiệm |
| I'd like to understand the path forward | Tôi muốn hiểu lộ trình phát triển |
| What would it take to get to the next level? | Cần gì để lên level tiếp theo? |
| I'm looking for a salary in the range of... | Tôi mong mức lương trong khoảng... |

## Nói "không" lịch sự — Nâng cao

| Câu | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| I want to help, but I'm at capacity | Tôi muốn giúp, nhưng tôi đã hết bandwidth |
| That's not something I can commit to right now | Tôi không thể cam kết điều đó lúc này |
| If we add this, something else needs to come off | Nếu thêm điều này, cần bỏ bớt thứ khác |
| Let me get back to you on that | Để tôi phản hồi bạn sau |
| I'd need more time / resources to do that properly | Cần thêm thời gian/nguồn lực để làm đúng |

## Hội thoại mẫu — Đề xuất cải tiến process với sếp

> **Dung:** "Hey, do you have a moment? I'd like to propose something."
> **Manager:** "Sure, what's on your mind?"
> **Dung:** "I've noticed we spend a lot of time on manual regression every sprint. What if we invested in automating the top 20 critical flows? I think this could save us about 6 hours per sprint and reduce human error."
> **Manager:** "That sounds interesting. What would it take?"
> **Dung:** "About 2 sprints of setup time, and I'd need one dev to support the framework. The benefit is we'd have a stable safety net for every release going forward."
> **Manager:** "Would you be open to putting together a short proposal?"
> **Dung:** "Absolutely. I'll have something ready by end of week."

## Hội thoại mẫu — Thương lượng scope với PM

> **PM:** "Can we add the export feature to this sprint?"
> **Dung:** "I want to help, but we're already at capacity with the payment flow testing. If we add export, we'd need to push either the performance test or the regression suite."
> **PM:** "Which one has less impact?"
> **Dung:** "Performance test is lower risk this sprint. We can phase it to next sprint. But I want to be transparent — that's a risk we're accepting."
> **PM:** "Understood. Let's move performance to next sprint then."
> **Dung:** "Got it, I'll update the plan. Can we document this decision so it's visible to the team?"

## Hội thoại mẫu — Xin tăng lương

> **Dung:** "I'd like to find some time to discuss my compensation — would that be okay?"
> **Manager:** "Of course. What's on your mind?"
> **Dung:** "Over the past year, I've taken on additional responsibilities — managing two QC members, leading the AI adoption initiative, and building the quality dashboard. Based on those contributions, I believe my current salary no longer reflects the scope of my role."
> **Manager:** "I appreciate you bringing this up directly. What range are you thinking?"
> **Dung:** "I'm looking for something in the range of X to Y, which I feel is aligned with the market for this level."
> **Manager:** "Let me look into it and get back to you by end of next week."
> **Dung:** "Thank you. I'd also love to understand what the path forward looks like for the next level, if that's something we can discuss."

## Tip thực tế

**Cấu trúc đề xuất hiệu quả:**
```
1. Problem:   "I've noticed..."
2. Solution:  "What if we..."
3. Benefit:   "This would help us..."
4. Ask:       "Would you be open to...?"
```

**Khi bị từ chối — giữ cửa mở:**
> "I understand. Could we revisit this next quarter?"
> "What would need to change for this to be considered?"

**Thương lượng không phải chiến thắng — đó là tìm điểm chung:**
> "I want to find something that works for both of us."
