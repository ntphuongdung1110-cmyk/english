# Idioms — Hằng Ngày & Công Việc
**Học 2–3 idiom/tuần. Dùng trong ghi âm buổi sáng.**

---

## Nhóm 1: Về Thời Gian & Deadline

| Idiom | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **against the clock** | đua với thời gian | We're working against the clock to fix this before launch. |
| **at the last minute** | vào phút chót | They changed the requirements at the last minute — again. |
| **ahead of schedule** | trước kế hoạch | We're ahead of schedule — testing finishes tomorrow. |
| **behind schedule** | chậm hơn kế hoạch | The dev team is behind schedule, so QC is under pressure. |
| **in the nick of time** | vừa đúng lúc, kịp thời | We caught the bug in the nick of time — 2 hours before release. |
| **right on track** | đúng tiến độ | We're right on track for Friday's go-live. |
| **buy time** | trì hoãn để có thêm thời gian | We need to buy some time before the next release. |
| **on the back burner** | tạm gác lại | That feature has been on the back burner for months. |

## Nhóm 2: Về Vấn Đề & Giải Quyết

| Idiom | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **hit a wall** | bị tắc, không tiến được | We hit a wall with the automation — the framework doesn't support it. |
| **get to the bottom of** | tìm ra nguyên nhân gốc | We need to get to the bottom of why this keeps breaking. |
| **a silver lining** | mặt tích cực trong tình huống xấu | The silver lining is we found 10 bugs before users did. |
| **go back to square one** | bắt đầu lại từ đầu | The approach didn't work — we had to go back to square one. |
| **a band-aid solution** | giải pháp tạm thời | That fix is just a band-aid — the root cause is still there. |
| **nip something in the bud** | giải quyết sớm khi còn nhỏ | We need to nip this performance issue in the bud. |
| **in the same boat** | cùng chung hoàn cảnh | Dev and QC are in the same boat — both under pressure. |
| **cut corners** | làm ẩu, bỏ qua bước | We can't cut corners on testing just to hit the deadline. |

## Nhóm 3: Về Giao Tiếp & Quan Hệ

| Idiom | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **on the same page** | đồng thuận, hiểu nhau | Let's make sure we're all on the same page before we start. |
| **read between the lines** | hiểu ý bên trong | When the PM said "we'll figure it out" — read between the lines. |
| **break the ice** | mở đầu, làm quen | I always try to break the ice with a question before meetings. |
| **get the ball rolling** | bắt đầu tiến hành | Let's get the ball rolling on the test plan for next sprint. |
| **take something on board** | tiếp thu / ghi nhớ ý kiến | Thanks for the feedback — I'll take that on board. |
| **touch base** | liên lạc / kiểm tra nhanh | I'll touch base with the dev team this afternoon. |
| **keep someone in the loop** | cập nhật thông tin cho ai | Keep me in the loop on the bug investigation. |
| **give someone the green light** | cho phép tiến hành | The PM gave us the green light to release. |

## Nhóm 4: Về Chất Lượng & Công Việc

| Idiom | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **raise the bar** | nâng cao tiêu chuẩn | This release raised the bar for our QC process. |
| **up to scratch** | đạt tiêu chuẩn | The test coverage isn't up to scratch yet. |
| **the tip of the iceberg** | chỉ là bề mặt vấn đề | These 3 bugs are just the tip of the iceberg. |
| **a double-edged sword** | lợi bất cập hại | Fast releases are a double-edged sword for quality. |
| **fire fighting** | xử lý khủng hoảng liên tục | We spend too much time firefighting instead of preventing. |
| **low-hanging fruit** | việc dễ làm nhất | Let's fix the low-hanging fruit first — quick wins. |
| **drop the ball** | mắc sai lầm / quên việc | We dropped the ball on that regression test last sprint. |
| **hit the nail on the head** | nói đúng vào vấn đề | You hit the nail on the head — the issue is in the API layer. |

## Nhóm 5: Daily Life Idioms (Casual)

| Idiom | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **hang in there** | cố lên, kiên nhẫn | It's a tough week, but hang in there. |
| **at the end of the day** | xét cho cùng | At the end of the day, quality matters more than speed. |
| **once in a while** | thỉnh thoảng | Once in a while, I take a full day off — no screens. |
| **sleep on it** | suy nghĩ qua đêm trước khi quyết định | Let me sleep on it — I'll give you an answer tomorrow. |
| **have a lot on one's plate** | nhiều việc phải làm | I have a lot on my plate this week — can we reschedule? |
| **in the long run** | về lâu dài | In the long run, automation saves us more time. |
| **go the extra mile** | cố gắng hơn mức cần thiết | The team went the extra mile to finish testing early. |
| **keep one's head above water** | vừa đủ để không bị nhấn chìm | We're barely keeping our heads above water this sprint. |
