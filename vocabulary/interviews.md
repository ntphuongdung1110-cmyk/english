## Hội thoại mẫu — Giới thiệu bản thân

> **Interviewer:** "Tell me about yourself."
>
> **Dung:** "Sure! I'm currently working as a QC Manager with about 5 years of experience in software quality. My background is in manual and automation testing, and I specialize in QC process improvement and team management. Recently, I've been leading an AI adoption initiative within our QC team — using AI tools to speed up test coverage and reporting. I'm passionate about building quality into the development process, not just catching bugs at the end. I'm looking for a role where I can take on more ownership and drive quality strategy at a product level."

---

## Hội thoại mẫu — Mô tả dự án thực tế

> **Interviewer:** "Can you walk me through a project you're proud of?"
>
> **Dung:** "Sure. One project I'm really proud of is building an internal QC dashboard. I noticed the team was spending too much time manually tracking bug counts and sprint quality metrics. I collaborated with a developer to build a Redmine-integrated dashboard that automated all of that. It reduced reporting time by about 80% and gave management real-time visibility into quality trends. The key challenge was defining the right metrics — I worked closely with the PM and dev lead to align on what actually mattered."

---

## Hội thoại mẫu — Câu hỏi tình huống (STAR method)

> **Interviewer:** "Tell me about a time you had a conflict with a developer."
>
> **Dung:** "Sure. In one sprint, a developer pushed back on a bug I raised — he felt it was a minor UI issue, not worth delaying the release. *(Situation)* I understood his concern about the timeline, but the bug was affecting the checkout experience on mobile. *(Task)* I prepared a short screen recording showing the actual user impact and brought it to our daily standup. *(Action)* Once the team saw the real-world effect, we agreed to fix it before release. *(Result)* It turned out to be a significant issue — our PM later told me it would have impacted conversion. The key takeaway for me was: show, don't just tell."

---

## STAR Method — Cấu trúc trả lời

```
S — Situation:  Bối cảnh là gì?
T — Task:       Nhiệm vụ/trách nhiệm của bạn?
A — Action:     Bạn đã làm gì cụ thể?
R — Result:     Kết quả ra sao? (có số liệu càng tốt)
```

## Tip thực tế

**Khi cần thêm thời gian suy nghĩ:**
> "That's a great question. Let me think for a moment..."

**Khi không hiểu câu hỏi:**
> "Could you clarify what you mean by...?"

**Kết thúc phỏng vấn:**
> "Thank you so much for your time. I really enjoyed learning more about the team and the role. I'm very excited about this opportunity."
