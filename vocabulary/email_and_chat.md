## Email mẫu — Follow-up sau họp

> **Subject:** Follow-up: Sprint Review Meeting — April 17
>
> Hi team,
>
> Following up on today's meeting. Here's a quick summary:
>
> **Decisions:**
> - Release pushed to April 19
> - Critical bugs assigned to Dev team (due EOD April 18)
>
> **Action items:**
> - [Dev] Fix checkout bug — April 18
> - [QC] Run smoke test — April 19, 9am
> - [Me] Send go/no-go by 10am
>
> Please let me know if I missed anything. Looking forward to a smooth release!
>
> Best,
> Dung

>
> Best,

## Email mẫu — Escalate vấn đề với sếp

> **Subject:** Heads Up: Risk on Release Timeline
>
> Hi [Manager],
>
> I'm reaching out to flag a potential risk. We found a critical bug in the payment flow late yesterday. Dev is working on it, but there's a chance we may need to push the release by one day.
>
> I'll keep you updated as soon as we have more clarity — likely by EOD today.
>
> Please let me know if you'd like to discuss.
>
> Best,
> Dung

## Tone guide — Formal vs Casual

| Tình huống | Dùng tone |
|---|---|
| Email cho sếp / stakeholder | Formal |
| Email nội bộ team | Semi-formal |
| Slack với teammate | Casual |
| Slack với sếp | Semi-formal |

```
Formal:   "I would appreciate your feedback at your earliest convenience."
Semi:     "Let me know your thoughts when you get a chance."
Casual:   "What do you think? 👀"
```
