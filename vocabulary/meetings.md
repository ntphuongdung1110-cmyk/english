## Hội thoại mẫu — Họp team QC

**Mở đầu:**
> A *(QC Manager)*: "Alright, let's get started. Can everyone hear me okay?"
> B: "Yep, loud and clear."
> A: "Great. We have three things on the agenda today — sprint review, bug status, and the release plan."

**Đưa ý kiến & phản biện:**
> A: "Looking at the bug count, I think we need to push the release by one day."
> B: "I see your point, but the dev team is pretty confident they can fix the critical ones by tonight."
> A: "That's fair. Have we considered running a quick smoke test first thing tomorrow morning?"
> B: "That works for me."

**Làm rõ:**
> C: "Sorry, just to clarify — are we talking about the staging or production build?"
> A: "Good question. Staging first, then we sign off for production."

**Tóm tắt action items:**
> A: "Okay, so to summarize: dev fixes critical bugs tonight, QC runs smoke test tomorrow at 9am, and I'll send the go/no-go by 10am. Who's taking ownership of the smoke test checklist?"
> B: "I'll handle that."
> A: "Perfect. I'll send out the meeting notes after this. Thanks everyone, let's wrap up here."

## Tip thực tế

**Khi không hiểu kịp:**
> "Sorry, I missed that last part — could you say it again?"

**Khi cần thời gian suy nghĩ:**
> "That's a good point. Let me think about it for a second."

**Khi muốn ngắt lời lịch sự:**
> "Sorry to jump in, but..." / "If I could just add something quickly..."
