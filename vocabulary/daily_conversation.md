## Small Talk với đồng nghiệp

| Từ/Cụm | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|---|---|---|
| How's it going? | Dạo này thế nào? | "Hey, how's it going?" |
| Not bad, you? | Cũng ổn, còn bạn? | — |
| Same old, same old | Vẫn vậy thôi | "How's work?" → "Same old, same old" |
| Hanging in there | Cố gắng cầm cự | "Busy week?" → "Yeah, hanging in there" |
| TGIF! | Cuối tuần rồi! | (Thank God It's Friday) |
| Any plans for the weekend? | Cuối tuần có kế hoạch gì không? | — |
| I could use a coffee | Tôi cần uống cà phê quá | — |
| Crazy busy lately | Dạo này bận điên lên | — |
| Let's catch up soon | Hẹn nói chuyện sớm nhé | — |

### Hội thoại mẫu — Small Talk

> A: "Hey, how's it going?"
> B: "Not bad! A bit tired — crazy busy week. You?"
> A: "Same here. TGIF though!"
> B: "Right? Any plans for the weekend?"
> A: "Might just stay home and recharge. I could really use the rest."

## Đặt vé / Booking

| Từ/Cụm | Nghĩa |
|---|---|
| I'd like to book... | Tôi muốn đặt... |
| Do you have availability on...? | Còn chỗ vào ngày... không? |
| I'll take the window seat | Tôi lấy ghế cửa sổ |
| Is it refundable? | Có hoàn tiền không? |
| Can I change the date? | Tôi có thể đổi ngày không? |
| What's included? | Bao gồm những gì? |
| I'd like to confirm my reservation | Tôi muốn xác nhận đặt chỗ |
| How much is the cancellation fee? | Phí hủy là bao nhiêu? |

### Hội thoại mẫu — Đặt vé máy bay

> A: "Hi, I'd like to book a flight to Singapore on April 25th."
> B: "Sure! One-way or round trip?"
> A: "Round trip, please. Returning on May 2nd."
> B: "We have seats available. Window or aisle?"
> A: "Window, please. Is the ticket refundable?"
> B: "Yes, but there's a cancellation fee of $30."
> A: "Got it. I'll go ahead and confirm the booking."

## Nhà hàng

| Tình huống | Câu |
|---|---|
| Vào nhà hàng | "Table for two, please" |
| Hỏi menu | "Could we see the menu?" |
| Gọi món | "I'll have the..." / "I'd like to try..." |
| Hỏi gợi ý | "What do you recommend?" |
| Dị ứng | "I'm allergic to..." / "Does this contain...?" |
| Gọi thêm | "Can we get another round?" |
| Tính tiền | "Check, please" / "Can we get the bill?" |
| Chia bill | "Can we split the bill?" |
| Khen món | "This is delicious!" / "Compliments to the chef" |

### Hội thoại mẫu — Nhà hàng

> A: "Hi, table for two, please."
> B: "Right this way! Here's the menu."
> A: "Thanks. What do you recommend today?"
> B: "The grilled salmon is very popular. It comes with vegetables and rice."
> A: "Sounds good! I'll have that. And does the pasta contain any dairy? I'm lactose intolerant."
> B: "I'll check with the kitchen for you."
> A: "I appreciate it, thank you."

*(Cuối bữa)*
> A: "Everything was delicious! Can we get the bill, please?"
> B: "Of course! Will you be splitting the bill?"
> A: "Yes, please — two separate checks."

> A: "Is it within walking distance from there?"
> B: "Yeah, maybe 10 minutes on foot. It's a nice walk too!"
> A: "Perfect, thank you so much!"

> B: "Your room is ready, actually! Here's your key card."
> A: "That's great, thank you!"

## Nói chuyện với chuyên gia / Giáo viên

### Hội thoại mẫu — Xin tư vấn

> A: "Hi, I was hoping to get your advice on something."
> B: "Of course, what's on your mind?"
> A: "I'm trying to improve my English for work — especially in meetings. Where should I start?"
> B: "I'd focus on active listening first — make sure you fully understand before responding."
> A: "That makes sense. Any resources you'd recommend?"
> B: "Podcasts are great for natural speech. Try listening daily, even just 15 minutes."
> A: "I'll give that a shot. Thanks for the tip!"

### Hội thoại mẫu — Hỏi giáo viên sau giờ học

> A: "Excuse me, do you have a moment?"
> B: "Sure, what's up?"
> A: "I'm struggling with the grammar point from today's lesson. Could you explain it again?"
> B: "Absolutely! Which part was confusing?"
> A: "The difference between 'used to' and 'would.' They seem similar."
> B: "Good question. 'Used to' covers both habits and states. 'Would' only works for habits."
> A: "Ah, that clears it up! Thank you so much."

## Review (Nhà hàng / Khách sạn / Khóa học)

### Cấu trúc dễ dùng

```
The [good thing] was great/amazing/perfect.
The only downside was [bad thing].
Overall, I'd [recommend it / go back / do it again].
```

### Ví dụ

**Review nhà hàng (nói với bạn bè):**
> "Honestly, the food was amazing — especially the beef. But the service was a bit slow. I'd still go back though, the vibe was really nice."

**Review khách sạn:**
> "The location was perfect — right in the city center. The room was clean and cozy. Only downside was the Wi-Fi was a bit spotty. Overall, worth the price."

**Review khóa học / giáo viên:**
> "The teacher was really patient and explained things clearly. The pace was good — not too fast, not too slow. I'd definitely recommend this class to anyone starting out."

## Lộ trình học tiếp theo

```
Tuần 1–2:  Meetings + Feedback
Tuần 3–4:  Email/Chat + Thương lượng
Tháng 2:   Phỏng vấn + Giải thích kỹ thuật
Tháng 3+:  Networking + Presentation
```

### Kịch bản ưu tiên (theo vai trò QC Manager)

1. **Họp & Cuộc họp** — mở đầu, kết thúc, đưa ý kiến, tóm tắt action items
2. **Feedback & Review** — cho/nhận feedback lịch sự, escalate vấn đề
3. **Email & Chat** — Slack/Teams, follow-up, nhắc nhở khéo léo
4. **Phỏng vấn** — giới thiệu bản thân, mô tả kinh nghiệm
5. **Thương lượng** — đề xuất ý tưởng, nói "không" lịch sự
6. **Giải thích kỹ thuật** — cho người không biết tech
