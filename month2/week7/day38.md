## 📚 Từ Vựng — Describing Trends & Data

### Xu Hướng Tăng

| Từ / Cụm | Phiên âm | Nghĩa |
|---|---|---|
| **increased by [X]%** | /ɪnˈkriːst baɪ [X] pɜːrsɛnt/ | Tăng X% |
| **rose to [number]** | /roʊz tuː [ˈnʌmbər]/ | Tăng lên |
| **jumped from [X] to [Y]** | /dʒʌmpt frɒm [X] tuː [Y]/ | Nhảy vọt từ X lên Y |
| **improved significantly** | /ɪmˈpruːvd sɪɡˈnɪfɪkəntli/ | Cải thiện đáng kể |
| **surged / spiked** | /sɜːrdʒd / spaɪkt/ | Tăng đột biến |
| **is on an upward trend** | /ɪz ɒn ən ˈʌpwərd trɛnd/ | Đang theo xu hướng tăng |

### Xu Hướng Giảm

| Từ / Cụm | Phiên âm | Nghĩa |
|---|---|---|
| **decreased by [X]%** | /diːˈkriːst baɪ [X] pɜːrsɛnt/ | Giảm X% |
| **dropped from [X] to [Y]** | /drɒpt frɒm [X] tuː [Y]/ | Rớt từ X xuống Y |
| **declined over the quarter** | /dɪˈklaɪnd ˈoʊvər ðə ˈkwɔːrtər/ | Giảm trong quý |
| **fell below [threshold]** | /fɛl bɪˈloʊ [ˈθrɛʃhoʊld]/ | Rớt dưới ngưỡng |
| **is on a downward trend** | /ɪz ɒn ə ˈdaʊnwərd trɛnd/ | Xu hướng giảm |

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu — Presenting QC Dashboard

```
"As you can see on this chart, our bug discovery rate 
jumped from 12 per sprint to 18 — 
that's a 50% increase over the last 3 sprints.

Now, before you worry — this is actually a good sign.
What it tells us is that our testers are getting better 
at catching issues earlier. More bugs found in QA 
means fewer bugs in production.

However, what is concerning is this line here — 
our average bug resolution time. 
It's been increasing steadily — 
up from 1.2 days to 2.1 days per bug.
That suggests the dev team may be stretched thin.

The takeaway: we're finding more bugs, 
but fixing them is taking longer. 
I'd recommend we look at whether we need 
to adjust the sprint scope for next cycle."
```

## 💬 Speaking Prompt (Ghi Âm 3 Phút)

**Dùng số liệu giả này để present:**
```
Pass rate:    Sprint 1: 72% → Sprint 2: 78% → Sprint 3: 85%
Bugs found:   Sprint 1: 23 → Sprint 2: 19 → Sprint 3: 14
Severity 1:   Sprint 1: 5 → Sprint 2: 2 → Sprint 3: 1
Avg fix time: Sprint 1: 1.5 days → Sprint 2: 1.8 days → Sprint 3: 2.2 days
```

Trình bày xu hướng, giải thích ý nghĩa, đưa ra recommendation.
