## Ôn Tập Tuần 7 — Presentation Skills

```
PREP METHOD:
□ Point → Reason → Example → Point
□ "The bottom line is..." / "If I had to leave you with one thing..."

DESCRIBING DATA:
□ "jumped from X to Y" / "dropped by X%"
□ "This is significant because..." / "What this tells us is..."
□ "To put that in context..."

DATA STORYTELLING:
□ "Let me paint a picture of..."
□ "The story behind the numbers is..."
□ "What this means in practice is..."

Q&A:
□ "Great question. [Direct answer]."
□ "I don't have that on hand — let me get back to you by EOD."
□ "Just to make sure I understand your question..."
□ "Happy to take this offline."
```
