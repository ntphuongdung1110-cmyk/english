## 📚 Từ Vựng — Q&A Language

| Tình Huống | Câu Nói |
|---|---|
| Câu hỏi dễ | "Great question. [Direct answer]." |
| Câu hỏi phức tạp | "That's a nuanced question. Let me think through it." |
| Không biết câu trả lời | "I don't have that data on hand — let me get back to you by EOD." |
| Câu hỏi ngoài phạm vi | "That's a bit outside the scope of today's presentation — happy to discuss after." |
| Câu hỏi aggressive | "I understand the concern. Let me address that directly." |
| Cần clarify câu hỏi | "Just to make sure I understand — are you asking about [X] or [Y]?" |
| Câu hỏi đã trả lời rồi | "I actually touched on this earlier — [brief recap]. Does that help?" |
| Kết thúc Q&A | "We're at time — but I'm happy to continue the conversation after." |

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu — Handling Tough Questions

### Khi Bị Challenge
```
"That's a fair challenge. Let me address it directly.

[Your evidence/reasoning]

I understand that might not be the answer you were hoping for, 
but based on the data, that's where I stand."
```

### Khi Bị Hỏi Quá Sâu Vào Chi Tiết
```
"Good detail question. I have the breakdown, 
but it might be more useful to take this offline 
so I can walk you through the full analysis.
Can we schedule 15 minutes after this?"
```

### Khi Không Chắc Câu Trả Lời
```
"Honestly, I'd rather give you accurate data 
than a quick answer that might be wrong.
Let me pull the exact numbers and share them by EOD today."
```

## 🎧 Shadowing Script — Q&A Session

```
"Happy to take questions.

[Q: "Why is the bug resolution time increasing?"]
Great question — and it's something I want to address directly.
The short answer is: we're finding more complex bugs now.
As our test coverage has improved, we're catching deeper issues — 
ones that require more investigation to fix.
So the resolution time increase is partly a sign of progress.
That said, I'm also watching this metric closely — 
if it keeps climbing, we'll need to look at dev capacity.

[Q: "What's your confidence level in this release?"]
I'd say 85%. We've covered all critical paths.
The remaining 15% uncertainty is around 3 edge cases 
we haven't fully tested under load conditions.
I'll have those results by end of day tomorrow.

[Q: "What if we hit a critical bug on Saturday?"]
Good scenario to plan for. We have a hotfix process ready.
I'll be available on Slack and can pull the team together 
within an hour if needed.
I don't expect it — but we're prepared."
```

## 💬 Speaking Prompt (Ghi Âm 3 Phút)

> **Sau presentation, nhận 4 câu hỏi (tự đặt và tự trả lời):**
> 1. Câu hỏi dễ (về data)
> 2. Câu hỏi phức tạp (về strategy)
> 3. Câu hỏi aggressive (challenge your approach)
> 4. Câu hỏi không biết trả lời
