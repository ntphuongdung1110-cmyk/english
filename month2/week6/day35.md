## 🎭 3 Tình Huống Roleplay

### Tình Huống 1 (10 phút)
> **"A high-performing tester has become increasingly negative in team meetings — sighing, eye-rolling, and occasionally making sarcastic comments when others suggest ideas. You need to address the attitude while keeping them motivated."**

Yêu cầu:
- Acknowledge their value first
- Name the behavior specifically (không nói "your attitude")
- Explain impact on team culture
- Ask about what's going on underneath
- Set clear expectation

---

### Tình Huống 2 (10 phút)
> **"You discover that a tester has been approving test cases without fully running them — to meet sprint deadlines. This is a trust and quality issue. Address it."**

Yêu cầu:
- Start with facts, not accusations
- Get their side of the story
- Explain the severity clearly
- Set non-negotiable standard
- Agree on a path forward

---

### Tình Huống 3 (10 phút)
> **"Your manager gives you feedback that 'the QC team has been slowing down releases' — but you believe the real problem is unclear requirements from product. How do you receive AND push back on this feedback professionally?"**

Yêu cầu:
- Receive the feedback without getting defensive
- Acknowledge the valid part
- Share your perspective with data
- Propose a collaborative solution

---

## 📝 Tip Trước Khi Ghi Âm

Đừng chuẩn bị script. Chỉ ghi nhớ:
```
1. Tên tình huống
2. SBI structure
3. Key phrase: "I want to be direct" / "Help me understand" / "Non-negotiable"
4. Close: "What do you need from me?"
```
