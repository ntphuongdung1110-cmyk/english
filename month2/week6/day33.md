## 📚 Từ Vựng Hôm Nay

| Tình Huống | Câu Phản Hồi Tự Nhiên |
|---|---|
| Nhận positive feedback | "Thank you — I really appreciate you saying that." |
| Nhận constructive feedback | "That's useful feedback — I'll think about how to improve." |
| Khi đồng ý | "You're right — I should have flagged that earlier." |
| Khi cần clarify trước khi phản hồi | "Could you give me a specific example? I want to make sure I understand." |
| Khi không đồng ý nhưng lịch sự | "I appreciate the feedback. I see it a bit differently — can I share my perspective?" |
| Khi cần thời gian | "Thank you for sharing this. Let me reflect on it and come back to you." |
| Khi muốn hỏi gì cần làm khác | "What would you like to see me do differently going forward?" |

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu — Receiving Feedback Framework

### Khi Feedback Đúng
```
"You're right, and I appreciate you being direct.
[Specific acknowledgment of the issue].
Going forward, I'll [specific action].
Is there anything else I should know?"
```

### Khi Muốn Clarify Trước
```
"Thank you for bringing this up.
Before I respond, could you help me understand [specific aspect]?
I want to make sure I'm addressing the right thing."
```

### Khi Có Góc Nhìn Khác
```
"I hear you, and I take this seriously.
I do see it a bit differently — 
[your perspective with specific evidence].
Would it be helpful to look at this together?"
```

## 🎧 Shadowing Script

```
"Thanks for that — I genuinely appreciate you telling me directly.

I'll be honest: my first instinct is to get defensive. 
But I know that's not useful.

So let me try to hear you properly. 
When you say the QC reports are hard to act on — 
could you point me to a specific example? 
I want to understand exactly what's missing.

Okay. I see it now. 
You're right — I was including technical details 
that the PM doesn't need, and missing the 'so what.'

That's a fair call. Going forward, 
I'll restructure the executive summary 
to lead with impact, not process.

And I'll send you a draft before the next review — 
would you be willing to give me quick feedback on that?"
```

## 💬 Speaking Prompt (Ghi Âm 2 Phút)

> **Tình huống:** PM nói với bạn: "Your QC updates in the all-hands are too technical — the leadership team doesn't follow. Can you simplify them?"

Nhận feedback tự nhiên: acknowledge → clarify → commit to action.
