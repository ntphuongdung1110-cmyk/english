## 📚 Từ Vựng Hôm Nay — Advanced Feedback

| Cụm | Phiên âm | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|---|---|
| **pattern I've been noticing** | /ˈpætərn aɪv bɪn ˈnoʊtɪsɪŋ/ | xu hướng lặp lại | There's a pattern I've been noticing over the last few sprints. |
| **it's becoming a concern** | /ɪts bɪˈkʌmɪŋ ə kənˈsɜːrn/ | đang trở thành vấn đề | This is becoming a concern for me. |
| **impact on the team** | /ˈɪmpækt ɒn ðə tiːm/ | ảnh hưởng đến team | I want to talk about the impact on the team. |
| **let's get ahead of this** | /lets ɡet əˈhed əv ðɪs/ | xử lý trước khi nghiêm trọng hơn | I want to get ahead of this before it becomes a bigger issue. |
| **performance improvement** | /pərˈfɔːrməns ɪmˈpruːvmənt/ | cải thiện hiệu suất | I want to support your growth and performance improvement. |
| **I need to see [change] by [date]** | /aɪ niːd tə siː/ | kỳ vọng rõ ràng | I need to see consistent documentation by end of next sprint. |
| **non-negotiable** | /nɒn nɪˈɡoʊʃiəbəl/ | không thể thương lượng | Accurate severity ratings — that's non-negotiable for me. |
| **what's getting in the way** | /wʌts ˈɡetɪŋ ɪn ðə weɪ/ | điều gì đang cản trở | Help me understand what's getting in the way. |

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu — Escalated Feedback

### Khi Vấn Đề Lặp Lại (Pattern)
```
"This is the [third] time we've talked about [issue]. 
I want to be direct because I think you're capable of better — 
and I need this to change.

[SBI — specific recent example]

Going forward, I need [specific expectation]. 
By [deadline]. 
Is there anything getting in the way of that?"
```

### Khi Ảnh Hưởng Đến Team
```
"I want to have a direct conversation, because what's happening 
is starting to affect the rest of the team.

When [behavior], it means [specific impact on others].
That's not fair to them — and I know that's not your intention.

So here's what I need: [clear expectation].
What do you need from me to make that happen?"
```

## 🎧 Shadowing Script

```
"I want to talk about something that's been on my mind.

Over the last three sprints, I've noticed a pattern 
where test cases are being marked as 'Pass' 
without actual execution — just based on assumptions.

I want to be clear: this is a serious issue. 
It means we're shipping bugs we think we've tested.
And it puts us at risk every single release.

I'm not here to make you feel bad — 
I genuinely believe you can do this well.
But I need this to change, starting now.

From next sprint: every test case needs execution evidence — 
a screenshot or log entry — before it can be marked Pass.
Non-negotiable.

Is there something making it difficult right now? 
I want to understand and I want to help.
But the expectation is clear."
```

## 💬 Speaking Prompt (Ghi Âm 3 Phút)
