# Day 31 — Giving Positive Feedback Effectively
**Thứ Hai | Tháng 2, Tuần 6**
**Chủ đề:** Feedback tích cực — cụ thể, chân thành, có tác động

---

## 📚 Từ Vựng Hôm Nay

| Từ / Cụm | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|
| **I wanted to call out** | I wanted to call out the great work on the regression suite. |
| **really stood out** | Your attention to detail really stood out this sprint. |
| **went above and beyond** | You went above and beyond with that test documentation. |
| **made a real difference** | Your early bug catch made a real difference to the release. |
| **I appreciate the effort** | I appreciate the effort you put into this. |
| **this is exactly what we need** | This level of coverage — this is exactly what we need. |
| **I've noticed** | I've noticed a significant improvement in your reports. |
| **keep it up** | Great work — keep it up. |

---

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu — Positive Feedback That Sticks

### Nguyên Tắc: Cụ Thể > Chung Chung
```
❌ "Good job this sprint."
✅ "I wanted to call out the way you documented the edge cases 
    in the payment flow — it was thorough, clear, and it helped 
    the dev team fix the bug 30% faster. That made a real difference."
```

### Pattern: Impact + Future
```
"[Specific action] — that made a real difference because [impact].
I'd love to see more of that going forward."
→ "The way you flagged that data integrity bug before it hit UAT — 
    that made a real difference because it saved us a full regression cycle.
    I'd love to see more of that proactive thinking going forward."
```

---

## 🎧 Shadowing Script

```
"Hey, I wanted to take a moment 
to give you some feedback — the good kind.

This sprint, I've noticed a real improvement 
in how you're structuring your bug reports. 
The reproduction steps are clear, the severity is accurate, 
and you're including context that helps the devs 
understand the impact right away.

That's not a small thing. It actually cut our 
average bug resolution time by about a day. 
So — really well done.

And I just wanted to make sure I said that 
explicitly, because I think good work 
deserves to be recognized.

Keep it up."
```
