## Từ Vựng Cốt Lõi Tháng 2

### Meeting & Facilitation
```
"Let's get the ball rolling."
"I'd like to open the floor to..."
"Let's put that in the parking lot."
"We're running short on time."
"Can we take this offline?"
"To recap what we've agreed on..."
"Next steps — [person] will [action] by [date]."
```

### Feedback Language (SBI Model)
```
S - Situation: "In today's meeting..."
B - Behavior: "I noticed that you [specific behavior]..."
I - Impact: "The impact of that was [effect]."
→ "In today's standup, I noticed you didn't mention the payment bug. 
    The impact was that the dev team didn't know to prioritize it."
```

### Presentation Signposting
```
"I'd like to start by..."          → mở đầu
"Moving on to..."                  → chuyển phần
"As you can see from this data..." → giới thiệu số liệu
"This is significant because..."   → giải thích tầm quan trọng
"To summarize..."                  → tóm tắt
"I'd like to leave you with..."    → kết bài
```
