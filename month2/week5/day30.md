## 📋 Ôn Tập Tuần 5 — Meeting Skills

```
OPENERS:
□ "Let's make this count."
□ "I want to keep us focused so we make the most of our time."
□ "I'll skip the small talk — we have a critical issue."

FACILITATION:
□ "I'd love to hear [Name]'s perspective."
□ "It sounds like there are two camps here: [A] and [B]."
□ "Let's try to bring this back to the main question."

ACTION ITEMS:
□ "[Person] will [task] by [date]."
□ "Does that sound right to everyone?"
□ "I'll send notes within the hour."

VIRTUAL:
□ "Can everyone hear me okay?"
□ "Sorry — I was on mute."
□ "Let me pull up the report — give me one second."
```
