## 🎯 Mục Tiêu Hôm Nay: Tốc Độ

Người Anh Mỹ nói trung bình **130–150 từ/phút**.
Người học thường nói **80–100 từ/phút**.

Hôm nay: cố gắng đẩy tốc độ lên, dù có sai một chút.
**Fluency > Accuracy** trong giai đoạn này.

---

## 🎭 3 Monologues — Không Chuẩn Bị, Không Dừng

### Monologue 1 (3 phút): The Rant
> **"Rant about one thing that frustrates you at work. Say everything you think — fast, casual, honest. No filter."**

Ví dụ mở đầu:
```
"Okay, I'm just going to say it — 
the thing that drives me crazy is..."
```

### Monologue 2 (3 phút): The Pitch
> **"Pitch your team to a new hire. Sell them on why your QC team is great to work with. Make it exciting."**

Ví dụ mở đầu:
```
"So, let me tell you about the team you're joining.
We're small but we're good. Really good. Here's why..."
```

### Monologue 3 (3 phút): The Opinion
> **"What do you think is the biggest mistake companies make when it comes to software quality? Go."**

Ví dụ mở đầu:
```
"Honestly? The biggest mistake I see over and over again is..."
```

```
Tốc độ của tôi: ___ từ/phút
```

---

## 🧠 Kỹ Thuật Tăng Tốc Độ Tự Nhiên

```
1. Dùng contractions:
   "I am" → "I'm"  |  "It is" → "It's"  |  "Do not" → "Don't"

2. Link words together:
   "a lot of" → "alotta"  |  "kind of" → "kinda"

3. Reduce unstressed syllables:
   "probably" → "prob'ly"  |  "because" → "'cause"  |  "going to" → "gonna"

4. Use shorter phrases:
   "In my opinion" → "Honestly" / "I think"
   "As a matter of fact" → "Actually" / "Look"
```
