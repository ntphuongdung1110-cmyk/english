## 🎙️ PHẦN 2 — NÓI (20 phút)

**Ghi âm 8 phút liên tục — 4 segments:**

### Segment 1 — Meeting (3 phút)
> "Open and run a sprint retrospective meeting. Agenda: what went well, what didn't, one action item."

Checklist:
```
□ Clear opening + outcome
□ Facilitate both sides
□ Action item: who / what / when
□ Natural close
```

### Segment 2 — Feedback (2 phút)
> "Give constructive feedback (SBI) to someone who consistently runs meetings without preparing an agenda."

Checklist:
```
□ SBI applied: Situation + Behavior + Impact
□ Specific, not vague
□ Forward-looking: clear expectation
□ Tone: direct but supportive
```

### Segment 3 — Presentation PREP (2 phút)
> "Use PREP to convince leadership to invest in test automation."

Checklist:
```
□ Point stated clearly
□ Reason with logic
□ Example: specific, real-sounding
□ Point restated with confidence
```

### Segment 4 — Q&A (1 phút)
> Handle: "Why should we trust automation over manual testing?"

Checklist:
```
□ Didn't get defensive
□ Acknowledged the concern
□ Gave evidence-based answer
□ Offered next step
```
