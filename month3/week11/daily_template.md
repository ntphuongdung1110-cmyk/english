## Day 61 — Abstract Topics

**Chủ đề:** Nói về chủ đề trừu tượng — technology, ethics, future of work

### Speaking Prompts (mỗi cái 3 phút)

```
1. "What impact do you think AI will have on software testing in the next 5 years?"

2. "Is it ethical to release software with known bugs if they're low severity?"

3. "Should QC be a separate team or embedded within dev teams? Why?"
```

### Vocabulary Cho Abstract Topics
```
"The implications of this are..."
"One could argue that..."
"There are two sides to this..."
"From a long-term perspective..."
"The real question is whether..."
"It raises the question of..."
"I think we need to distinguish between X and Y here."
```

## Day 62 — Debate Style (Argue Both Sides)

**Chủ đề:** Luyện khả năng lập luận — kể cả quan điểm bạn không đồng ý

### Topic: "Manual testing will become obsolete in 10 years."

```
Bài tập:
[Phút 1–2] Argue FOR: "Yes, manual testing will be obsolete because..."
[Phút 3–4] Argue AGAINST: "Actually, manual testing will always be needed because..."
[Phút 5]   Your REAL opinion: "Personally, I believe..."
```

**Phrases Cho Debate:**
```
FOR:   "The evidence suggests..." / "Consider the fact that..."
AGAINST: "However, one must also consider..." / "Critics would argue..."
REAL:   "Having considered both sides, I'm inclined to think..."
```

## Day 63 — Extended Storytelling (10 minutes)

**Chủ đề:** Kể 1 câu chuyện hoàn chỉnh trong 10 phút — không dừng

### Story Prompt
> **"Tell the story of your career journey — from where you started, key turning points, challenges, to where you are now as a QC Manager."**

Structure:
```
Phút 1–2:   Where you started (setting)
Phút 3–4:   First big challenge
Phút 5–6:   Turning point / key learning
Phút 7–8:   How you got to QC Manager
Phút 9–10:  What you've learned + where you're going
```

**Tip:** Dùng storytelling phrases từ Day 7. Không cần hoàn hảo — cần liên tục.

## Day 64 — Impromptu Speaking

**Chủ đề:** Nói về chủ đề ngẫu nhiên — không chuẩn bị gì cả

### Method: PREP in 10 seconds
Khi nghe chủ đề → trong 10 giây:
```
P: Decide your point
R: Pick 1 reason
E: Think of 1 example
→ Start talking!
```

### 5 Random Topics (chọn ngẫu nhiên bằng cách tung xúc xắc hoặc random number)
```
1. "The most important skill for a QC Manager"
2. "Working from home vs. in-office"
3. "The best book or podcast you've encountered recently"
4. "A time you made a mistake at work and what you learned"
5. "What would you change about how software is built today?"
```

**Rule:** Nhìn chủ đề → đếm 10 → bắt đầu nói. Không chuẩn bị thêm.

## Day 65 — Senior Role Interview Simulation

**Chủ đề:** Phỏng vấn vị trí QC Manager cấp cao — bằng tiếng Anh

### 8 Interview Questions (tự trả lời, mỗi câu 1.5–2 phút)

```
1. "Tell me about yourself and your QC background."
2. "What's your approach to building a QC process from scratch?"
3. "Describe a time you had to push back on a release decision."
4. "How do you handle conflict between QC and development teams?"
5. "What metrics do you use to measure quality?"
6. "How have you grown your team's automation capabilities?"
7. "What's your leadership philosophy?"
8. "Where do you see QA heading in the next 3–5 years?"
```

**Ghi âm toàn bộ. Nghe lại. Chọn câu trả lời tệ nhất và làm lại.**
