## 📚 Từ Vựng Hôm Nay — Meeting Management

| Từ / Cụm | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **set the agenda** | đặt chương trình | I'll send the agenda before the meeting. |
| **action item** | việc cần làm sau meeting | Let's capture that as an action item. |
| **parking lot** | vấn đề gác lại cho sau | Good point — let's park that for now. |
| **time-box** | giới hạn thời gian 1 mục | I'm going to time-box this to 10 minutes. |
| **facilitate** | dẫn dắt | I'll be facilitating today's session. |
| **take the floor** | được quyền nói | I'd like to take the floor on this. |
| **open the floor** | mời mọi người đóng góp ý kiến | Let me open the floor for questions. |
| **table a discussion** | tạm gác vấn đề | Can we table that for the next meeting? |
| **recap** | tóm tắt lại | Let me quickly recap what we've covered. |
| **next steps** | các bước tiếp theo | Let's align on next steps before we close. |

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu — Từ Mở Đến Đóng Meeting

### Mở Meeting
```
"Alright, let's get started. Thanks everyone for joining."
"I'll keep us moving — we have [X] items to cover today."
"Quick housekeeping: please hold questions until the end."
"Today's goal is to [outcome] by end of session."
```

### Giới Thiệu Agenda
```
"Today's agenda has three items: first [X], then [Y], and finally [Z]."
"I'm going to time-box each section to keep us on track."
"If we finish early, we can open the floor for anything else."
```

### Chuyển Giữa Các Mục
```
"Great. Let's move on to the next item."
"We're running a bit short on time — let's keep moving."
"I want to make sure we get to [important topic], so let's park that for now."
```

### Mời Ý Kiến
```
"I'd like to hear from [name] on this."
"Does anyone have a different perspective?"
"Any concerns before we move forward?"
"Let me open the floor — thoughts?"
```

### Đóng Meeting
```
"Let me quickly recap what we've agreed on."
"Action items: [person] will [action] by [date]."
"If nothing else, we're good to close. Thanks everyone."
"I'll send out meeting notes within the hour."
```

## 🎧 Shadowing Script — Full Meeting Flow

```
"Alright, let's get started. Thanks for joining — 
I know everyone's busy, so I'll keep this tight.

Today we have three items on the agenda. 
First, we'll go over the QC status for this sprint. 
Then we'll discuss the two open blockers. 
And finally, we'll align on the release decision.

I'm going to time-box each section to about 10 minutes, 
so let's stay focused.

Let's kick off with the QC status. 
[Dung], do you want to take the floor on that?

...

Good. Any questions on that before we move on? 
No? Great, let's move to the blockers.

...

Okay, we're coming up on time. Let me recap.
We've agreed to delay the release by 24 hours.
Action items: Dev team will fix the payment bug by 5pm tomorrow.
QC will do a targeted regression first thing Thursday morning.

If nothing else, we're good to close. 
Thanks everyone — I'll send notes shortly."
```

## 💬 Speaking Prompt — Roleplay (Ghi Âm 3 Phút)

> **Tình huống:** Bạn đang chạy sprint review meeting với 5 người (dev, PM, designer). Dẫn dắt từ đầu đến cuối.

**Cấu trúc:**
```
[0:00] Mở meeting + welcome
[0:30] Giới thiệu agenda (3 mục)
[1:00] Mục 1: QC update (tóm tắt nhanh)
[1:30] Mục 2: 1 blocker cần quyết định
[2:00] Mời ý kiến + quyết định
[2:30] Recap action items + đóng meeting
```
