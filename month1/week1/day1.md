## 📚 Từ Vựng Hôm Nay (10 từ/cụm)

| Từ / Cụm | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ |
|---|---|---|
| **currently** | hiện tại | I'm currently working as a QC Manager. |
| **responsible for** | chịu trách nhiệm về | I'm responsible for quality assurance. |
| **in charge of** | phụ trách | I'm in charge of a team of 5 testers. |
| **deal with** | xử lý / đối phó | I deal with critical bugs daily. |
| **collaborate with** | hợp tác với | I collaborate with the dev team closely. |
| **passionate about** | đam mê | I'm passionate about improving processes. |
| **background in** | nền tảng về | I have a background in software testing. |
| **specialize in** | chuyên về | I specialize in QC for mobile apps. |
| **look after** | quản lý / trông nom | I look after the entire test cycle. |
| **keen on** | hứng thú với | I'm keen on learning new testing tools. |

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu Hôm Nay

### Pattern 1: Giới thiệu kinh nghiệm
```
I've been working as [job title] for [X] years.
→ I've been working as a QC Manager for 3 years.
→ I've been in the software industry for over 5 years.
```

### Pattern 2: Mô tả công việc hiện tại
```
My main responsibility is [verb-ing / to + verb]
→ My main responsibility is ensuring product quality before release.
→ My main responsibility is to manage the QC team and test processes.
```

### Pattern 3: Mô tả team
```
I'm currently leading / managing / overseeing a team of [number] [people/testers/engineers]
→ I'm currently leading a team of 6 QC engineers.
→ I oversee a team that handles both manual and automation testing.
```

### Pattern 4: Nói về điểm mạnh
```
One thing I pride myself on is [noun / verb-ing]
→ One thing I pride myself on is attention to detail.
→ One thing I pride myself on is delivering clear, actionable bug reports.
```

### Pattern 5: Kết thúc tự giới thiệu tự nhiên
```
Outside of work, I [enjoy / like / spend time] [verb-ing]
→ Outside of work, I enjoy learning English and staying up to date with tech trends.
→ Outside of work, I like reading about software quality methodologies.
```

## 🎧 Shadowing Script

> Đọc đoạn này và bắt chước giọng, nhịp, ngữ điệu của người bản xứ. Lặp 5 lần.

```
"Hi, I'm [Dung]. I've been working in software quality 
for about [X] years now. Currently, I'm a QC Manager, 
which means I'm responsible for making sure our products 
are ready for release — no critical bugs, no surprises 
for our users.

On a daily basis, I work closely with developers, 
product managers, and designers. I lead a team of QC 
engineers, and honestly, the part I enjoy most is 
mentoring them and helping them grow.

Outside of work, I've been working on my English — 
I want to be able to communicate more naturally in 
international settings. That's why I'm here."
```

**Hướng dẫn shadowing:**
1. Đọc qua 1 lần để hiểu nghĩa
2. Đọc to chậm rãi, chú ý phát âm
3. Tăng tốc dần đến tốc độ tự nhiên
4. Ghi âm lần cuối và nghe lại

## 💬 Speaking Prompt (Ghi Âm 3 Phút)

Trả lời câu hỏi này bằng tiếng Anh, ghi âm lại:

> **"Tell me about yourself — your background, your current role, and what you enjoy about your work."**

**Gợi ý cấu trúc:**
```
Phần 1 (45 giây): Background — I've been in [field] for...
Phần 2 (60 giây): Current role — Currently I'm responsible for...
Phần 3 (45 giây): What I enjoy — The part I enjoy most is...
Phần 4 (30 giây): Outside work — Outside of work, I...
```

**Không cần hoàn hảo. Cứ nói liên tục.**
