## 📚 Từ Vựng Hôm Nay — Work Action Verbs

| Từ / Cụm | Phiên âm | Nghĩa | Ví Dụ Thực Tế |
|---|---|---|---|
| **kick off** | /kɪk ɒf/ | bắt đầu (meeting/ngày làm) | We kick off with a standup at 9am. |
| **wrap up** | /ræp ʌp/ | kết thúc | Let's wrap up by 5pm. |
| **check in with** | /tʃek ɪn wɪð/ | kiểm tra / hỏi thăm | I check in with my team every morning. |
| **go over** | /ɡoʊ ˈoʊvər/ | xem xét, review | I go over the bug list before the meeting. |
| **prioritize** | /praɪˈɔːrɪtaɪz/ | ưu tiên | We need to prioritize the critical issues. |
| **follow up on** | /ˈfɒloʊ ʌp ɒn/ | theo dõi tiến độ | I'll follow up on that ticket tomorrow. |
| **loop in** | /luːp ɪn/ | kéo ai vào vòng thông tin | Let me loop in the dev lead on this. |
| **keep an eye on** | /kiːp ən aɪ ɒn/ | theo dõi sát | I keep an eye on the test coverage metrics. |
| **get bogged down** | /ɡet bɒɡd daʊn/ | bị sa lầy / mất nhiều thời gian | I don't want to get bogged down in details. |
| **catch up on** | /kætʃ ʌp ɒn/ | cập nhật lại | I need to catch up on the test reports. |

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu Hôm Nay

### Pattern 1: Mô tả đầu ngày
```
First thing in the morning, I [verb]...
→ First thing in the morning, I check Slack and go over overnight bug reports.
→ First thing in the morning, I prioritize the day's test tasks with my team.
```

### Pattern 2: Mô tả hoạt động thường xuyên
```
Throughout the day, I [verb]...
→ Throughout the day, I coordinate between the QC team and developers.
→ Throughout the day, I review test cases and check on progress.
```

### Pattern 3: Mô tả cuối ngày
```
Towards the end of the day, I [verb]...
→ Towards the end of the day, I wrap up the test summary report.
→ At the end of the day, I check what's left for tomorrow and update the backlog.
```

### Pattern 4: Nói về tần suất
```
[Frequency adverb], I tend to [verb]
→ Usually, I tend to block out the first 30 minutes for deep focus work.
→ On Mondays, I tend to run a quick team sync to set the week's priorities.
```

### Pattern 5: Mô tả điều mình thích/không thích trong ngày
```
The [best/hardest/most rewarding] part of my day is [noun/verb-ing]
→ The most rewarding part of my day is when a release goes out clean.
→ The hardest part is managing last-minute bug reports right before a deadline.
```

## 🎧 Shadowing Script

```
"So, a typical day for me starts around 8:30. 
First thing I do is check Slack — see if there are 
any urgent issues overnight. Then I go over the test 
status from the day before.

At 9, we have a standup. I check in with my QC team, 
get updates on what they're testing, and flag any 
blockers to the dev team.

Mid-morning is usually my deep work time — I review 
test cases, go over bug reports, and make sure we're 
aligned on priorities.

Afternoons are more meeting-heavy. I loop in with 
product managers, discuss upcoming releases, and 
follow up on anything that got escalated.

By 5 or 5:30, I wrap up with a quick summary — 
what got done, what's still open, and what the team 
needs to tackle tomorrow."
```

**Luyện tập:**
- Lần 1: Đọc to, hiểu nghĩa từng câu
- Lần 2: Shadowing theo giọng tự nhiên
- Lần 3–5: Tăng tốc, bắt chước nhịp điệu

## 💬 Speaking Prompt (Ghi Âm 3 Phút)

> **"Walk me through a typical day in your role as QC Manager."**

**Cấu trúc gợi ý:**
```
08:00 – 09:00  →  What you do first
09:00 – 12:00  →  Morning activities
12:00 – 14:00  →  After lunch
14:00 – 17:30  →  Afternoon focus
End of day     →  How you wrap up
```

**Cố gắng dùng ít nhất 5 action verbs từ bảng từ vựng hôm nay.**

## 🔄 Connected Speech Cần Chú Ý Hôm Nay

```
"first thing" → nghe như "firs-thing" (t bị nuốt)
"kind of" → nghe như "kinda"
"going to" → nghe như "gonna"
"want to" → nghe như "wanna"
"check in with" → nghe liền thành 1 cụm
```
