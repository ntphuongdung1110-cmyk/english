## 🎙️ PHẦN 2 — NÓI (20 phút)

**Ghi âm 7 phút liên tục — 5 segments:**

### Segment 1 (1 phút): Self-Introduction
> "Introduce yourself as if meeting an international colleague for the first time."

### Segment 2 (2 phút): QC Status Update Roleplay
> "The PM asks: 'Can you give us a quick QC update for this sprint?'
> Report includes: test results, 1 critical bug, your recommendation."

### Segment 3 (2 phút): Handle Pushback
> "PM responds: 'We can't delay — we need to release tomorrow.'
> Handle this professionally. Disagree, propose compromise, stay calm."

### Segment 4 (1 phút): Career Goals
> "Someone asks: 'Where do you see yourself in 3 years?'"

### Segment 5 (1 phút): Close Naturally
> "End the conversation naturally with small talk and a warm close."

---

**Chấm điểm Phần 2:**
```
Segment 1 — Self-intro:
  □ Không dùng "I am" mở đầu
  □ Dùng được ít nhất 3 từ: responsible for / in charge of / passionate about
  □ Kết thúc tự nhiên
  Điểm: __ / 10

Segment 2 — QC Update:
  □ Dùng số liệu cụ thể
  □ Nêu bug rõ ràng (severity + description)
  □ Có recommendation
  □ QC terms: severity / test cases / regression / sign-off
  Điểm: __ / 10

Segment 3 — Handle Pushback:
  □ Không aggressive, không capitulate ngay
  □ Dùng: "I hear you, but...", "I'd push back on that"
  □ Đề xuất compromise cụ thể
  □ Giữ được relationship
  Điểm: __ / 10

Segment 4 — Career Goals:
  □ Short-term + long-term rõ ràng
  □ Dùng: "I'm looking to..." / "longer term, I see myself..."
  □ Mention 1 area for improvement
  Điểm: __ / 10

Segment 5 — Natural Close:
  □ Small talk câu cuối
  □ Warm closing phrase
  □ Không nghe abrupt
  Điểm: __ / 10

TỔNG PHẦN 2: ___ / 50
```
