## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu Hôm Nay

### Pattern 1: Mô tả phong cách lãnh đạo
```
"My leadership style is [style]. I believe [core principle]."
→ My leadership style is collaborative. I believe the best outcomes come 
   when the team feels safe to raise concerns early.
```

### Pattern 2: Nói về cách xây dựng team
```
"When I joined [company/team], one of the first things I did was [action].
My goal was to [purpose]."
→ When I took over the QC team, one of the first things I did was hold 
   1-on-1s with every member. My goal was to understand their strengths 
   and what was holding them back.
```

### Pattern 3: Nói về culture
```
"I try to foster a culture where [value].
What that looks like in practice is [concrete behavior]."
→ I try to foster a culture where quality is everyone's responsibility — 
   not just QC's. What that looks like in practice is dev and QC working 
   side by side from the start of each sprint.
```

### Pattern 4: Nói về challenge trong leadership
```
"One of the hardest parts of leading a team is [challenge].
What I've learned is [lesson]."
→ One of the hardest parts of leading a QC team is managing the tension 
   between speed and quality. What I've learned is that the conversation 
   gets easier when you have data to back you up.
```

## 🎧 Shadowing Script

```
"I've been managing QC teams for a few years now, 
and one thing I've learned is that 
the best QC culture isn't built through processes alone — 
it's built through trust.

My style is collaborative. I give my team autonomy, 
but with clear expectations. 
They know what good looks like, 
and I trust them to get there.

One thing I'm really focused on is psychological safety — 
making sure people feel comfortable raising issues 
early, without fear of blame. 
Because if someone hesitates to flag a risk, 
that's when things slip through.

I also believe in leading by example. 
If I expect the team to document their work thoroughly, 
I do the same with my own tasks. 
That consistency builds credibility."
```
