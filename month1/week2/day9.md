## 📚 Từ Vựng Hôm Nay

### Đồng Ý — Từ Mạnh → Nhẹ
```
"Absolutely."                   → Đồng ý hoàn toàn
"Exactly."                      → Đúng vậy
"That's a great point."         → Điểm hay
"I completely agree."           → Đồng ý hoàn toàn
"You're right about that."      → Bạn đúng
"Fair enough."                  → Có lý, chấp nhận được
"That makes sense."             → Nghe có lý
"I can see where you're coming from." → Tôi hiểu quan điểm của bạn
```

### Không Đồng Ý — Từ Nhẹ → Mạnh
```
"I see your point, but..."                    → Nhẹ nhất
"That's a fair point, though I wonder if..."  → Đặt câu hỏi
"I'm not sure I fully agree because..."       → Không chắc
"I'd push back on that a little."             → Phản đối nhẹ
"I have some concerns about that."            → Có lo ngại
"With all due respect, I think..."            → Lịch sự nhưng thẳng
"I actually disagree on this one."            → Rõ ràng không đồng ý
```

### Phrases Trung Lập — Vừa Đồng Ý Vừa Thêm Điểm
```
"Yes, and on top of that..."        → Đồng ý + thêm
"You make a good point. I'd also add that..." → Đồng ý + bổ sung
"That's true, though we should also consider..." → Đồng ý + khía cạnh khác
"I agree to some extent, but..."    → Đồng ý một phần
```

## 🗣️ Mẫu Câu Hôm Nay

### Pattern 1: Đồng ý + đóng góp thêm
```
"Absolutely — and I'd add that [additional point]."
→ Absolutely — and I'd add that we should also check the edge cases on mobile.

"That makes total sense. On top of that, we might also want to [action]."
→ That makes total sense. On top of that, we might also want to loop in the dev lead.
```

### Pattern 2: Không đồng ý một cách lịch sự
```
"I see your point, but I'm a little concerned about [issue]."
→ I see your point, but I'm a little concerned about the timeline — 2 days isn't enough for a full regression.

"That's fair, though I'd push back on [specific part]."
→ That's fair, though I'd push back on skipping the smoke test — that's too much risk.
```

### Pattern 3: Hỏi để clarify trước khi đồng ý/không đồng ý
```
"Just to make sure I understand — are you suggesting we [restate their point]?"
"Could you help me understand the rationale behind [decision]?"
→ Just to make sure I understand — are you suggesting we ship without regression testing?
→ Could you help me understand why we're skipping UAT this time?
```

### Pattern 4: Phản đối + đề xuất thay thế
```
"I hear you, but I think there might be a better way to approach this.
What if we [alternative]?"
→ I hear you, but I think there might be a better way. What if we do a targeted test 
   on just the affected modules instead of skipping testing altogether?
```

### Pattern 5: Chốt lại sau tranh luận
```
"I think we're aligned on [point], even if we see [other point] differently."
"Can we agree to [compromise/action]?"
→ I think we're aligned on the goal — ship quality software — even if we see 
   the timeline differently. Can we agree to a 12-hour extension?
```

## 🎧 Shadowing Script — Disagreement in Meeting

```
"Okay, so I hear what you're saying about the deadline, 
and I completely understand the pressure we're under.

That said, I do have some concerns. 
If we ship without running regression on the payment module, 
we're taking a real risk. And honestly? 
I'd push back on that.

I mean, last time we skipped regression, 
we ended up with a production issue that took three days to fix. 
I'm not saying that'll happen again, but...

I see your point that we're tight on time. 
What if we do this — we skip the low-risk areas 
and focus regression on just the payment and checkout flows? 
That would take maybe 4 hours instead of a full day.

Does that work? I think that's a fair middle ground."
```

## 💬 Speaking Prompt — Roleplay (Ghi Âm 3 Phút)

> **Tình huống:** PM nói: "Let's just skip regression testing this sprint — we're too behind on schedule."

Bạn không đồng ý. Hãy:
1. Thừa nhận áp lực deadline (đồng ý một phần)
2. Nêu rõ lo ngại của bạn (phản đối lịch sự)
3. Đề xuất compromise (giải pháp trung hòa)
4. Mở thảo luận

**Yêu cầu:** Dùng ít nhất 3 cụm "disagreement" và 2 cụm "agreement" từ bảng trên.

---

## 🧠 Insight Hôm Nay

**Người Mỹ / người bản xứ không nói "No, you're wrong."**
Họ dùng "soft pushback" — phản đối mà vẫn giữ được quan hệ tốt:

```
❌ "That's wrong. We can't skip testing."
✅ "I hear you — though I do have some concerns about skipping testing."

❌ "No, we must do regression."
✅ "I'd push back on that a little — what if we do a targeted regression instead?"
```
